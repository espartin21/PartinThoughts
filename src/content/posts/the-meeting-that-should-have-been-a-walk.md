---
title: "The meeting that should have been a walk"
date: 2025-08-19
tag: work
excerpt: "On the small heresy of suggesting you leave the building, together, with no agenda."
---

The most productive hour I had this quarter was a walk with a colleague where we explicitly agreed not to solve anything. We just described the shape of the problem to each other, out loud, at walking pace. By the end of it, the shape had changed.
