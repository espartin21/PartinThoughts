---
title: "The five o'clock thing"
date: 2025-01-29
tag: work
excerpt: "A habit I stole from a former boss that has quietly fixed my Fridays."
---

At five o'clock on Friday, spend ten minutes writing down the three things you actually moved that week. Not the meetings you attended. The things that moved. Most weeks it's a short list. That's fine. The list is how you know.
