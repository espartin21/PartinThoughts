title: "Winter light"
date: 2025-12-29
tag: personal
excerpt: "The specific way the sun hits the kitchen table for about eleven minutes in late December. Why I started noticing."
---

It comes in low through the west window, between 3:42 and 3:53, and it turns the whole room the color of a good peach. You have to be sitting still, ideally with tea. You cannot call it up on purpose. You can only be available when it arrives.

My friend Maya once told me the reason to have a quiet life is that it trains you to notice. I think she was right, but I don't think I understood it until this week.
