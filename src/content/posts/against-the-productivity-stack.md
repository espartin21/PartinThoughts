The stack is never the problem. The problem is that I keep reaching for tools when what I need is a decision. An app won't tell you whether to say no to the meeting; it will only make the yes more legible.

## What actually helps

A single index card on the desk, with three things, in pencil, that I'd be proud of finishing today. That is the entire system. Everything else is ambient.
