---
title: "A defense of boring hobbies"
date: 2025-03-11
tag: personal
excerpt: "Not every interest needs a side-hustle arc. Some of them are for being a person."
---

I knit. I am not good at it. I will never sell a scarf. The scarves are lumpy and they are for me, and the not-being-good-at-it is the entire point — it is the last thing in my life with no stakes, and I guard it.
